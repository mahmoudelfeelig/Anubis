### Kryptex Helix

The maintenance log shows the helix captured in chalk before the rings were realigned. The outer casing sketch survived in the margin:

```
┌───────┐
| S P I |
| E Y R |
| K L A |
└───────┘
```

Directly beneath it, the core lattice was scribbled as the technicians stepped inward:

```
      R
    I   N
  G   C   I
P   H   E   R
```

The crew noted, “Trace the coil from the rim toward the center—no jumps, no reversals.” Read the handles hidden in those paths and feed them back to the vault.
