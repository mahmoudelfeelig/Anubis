### Palimpsest Ledger

The curator’s memo mentions that the margin guidon still repeats the word “PALIMPSEST,” even after the illumination faded. Folded inside the ledger was a single line, written in a tight slate hand:

```
kewtgbumhatr|lkddkxavzej
```

They claimed running the text through the ledger’s old cipher desk would surface the two stewards who last touched the book. Recover those names and record them in order.
