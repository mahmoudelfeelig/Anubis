### Chronometric Vector

Nine timing outposts relayed their final heartbeat to the archive. Each entry was logged in the station’s own wall clock, together with the offset they kept from UTC.

Translate every tick into UTC, queue them from earliest to latest, and read the initials in that aligned order. Operations also noted that the offset column, once “recentred around zero”, spells the companion handle.
