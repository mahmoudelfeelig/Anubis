# LV-020 - Automata Flux

Starting at `S0` (label `a`), the automaton processes the bitstream `10110011101`:

| Step | Bit | State before | Label | Next state | Emit |
| ---- | --- | ------------- | ----- | ---------- | ---- |
| 0 | — | S0 | a | — | — |
| 1 | 1 | S0 | a | S1 | s |
| 2 | 0 | S1 | u | S2 | t |
| 3 | 1 | S2 | t | S3 | a |
| 4 | 1 | S3 | o | S4 | t |
| 5 | 0 | S4 | m | S5 | e |
| 6 | 0 | S5 | a | S6 | c |
| 7 | 1 | S6 | t | S7 | i |
| 8 | 1 | S7 | a | S8 | p |
| 9 | 1 | S8 | f | S9 | h |
| 10 | 0 | S9 | l | S10 | e |
| 11 | 1 | S10 | u | S11 | r |

Collecting the state labels (before each move, including the start) yields `automataflux`. Collecting the emitted symbols gives `statecipher`.
