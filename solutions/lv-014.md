# LV-014 - Residue Cartography

**Username via Chinese Remainders**

For each slice, solve the simultaneous congruences. The least non-negative solutions are:

- A: \(x ≡ 17 \pmod{7}\) and \(x ≡ 32 \pmod{5}\) ⇒ \(x = 17\).
- B: \(x ≡ 4 \pmod{8}\), \(x ≡ 11 \pmod{7}\) ⇒ \(x = 4\).
- C: \(x ≡ 36 \pmod{9}\), \(x ≡ 26 \pmod{4}\) ⇒ \(x = 18\).
- D: \(x ≡ 30 \pmod{11}\), \(x ≡ 23 \pmod{5}\) ⇒ \(x = 8\).
- E: \(x ≡ 3 \pmod{13}\), \(x ≡ 17 \pmod{7}\) ⇒ \(x = 3\).
- F: \(x ≡ 27 \pmod{7}\), \(x ≡ 38 \pmod{9}\) ⇒ \(x = 20\).
- G: \(x ≡ 28 \pmod{8}\), \(x ≡ 9 \pmod{5}\) ⇒ \(x = 4\).
- H: \(x ≡ 30 \pmod{9}\), \(x ≡ 22 \pmod{5}\) ⇒ \(x = 12\).
- I: \(x ≡ 30 \pmod{10}\), \(x ≡ 0 \pmod{3}\) ⇒ \(x = 0\).
- J: \(x ≡ 26 \pmod{11}\), \(x ≡ 33 \pmod{6}\) ⇒ \(x = 15\).

Mapping these integers with `0 → a` yields `residuemap`.
